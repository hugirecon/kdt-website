## Leadership Team

### Michael Schulz - CEO, Head of SPEAR Branch
Michael Schulz serves as the Chief Executive Officer of KDT and leads the SPEAR Branch. While Matthew McCalla leads the forces within and manages their operations, Michael's focus is directed externally. Driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence, he dedicates himself to making KDT a leader in innovation and enterprise, ensuring its growth benefits all KDT Personnel.

**Background:** Born into an all-American ranching family with German roots, hard work flows through his veins. Schulz's relentless work ethic has been shown in every aspect of his career. His style of work has made him well known in the industry as the one who lands contracts. During and after his education, he co-founded and contributed to various business ventures and community projects, excelling in roles such as community outreach, project coordination, and on-the-ground sales. Leveraging his experience as a trader, Michael founded and led a private investment group and simultaneously established himself in B2B sales. His primary interests are his church, his family and Private Security Contracting. He seeks rapid efficiency and "to make the system work".

**Michael Schulz seeks:**
- Rapid efficiency
- Effectiveness
- "To make the system work."

### Matthew McCalla - COO, Head of PSC Branch
Matthew McCalla serves as the Chief Operating Officer of KDT and leads the PSC Branch. While Michael Schulz leads the charge externally, Matthew's focus is on the internal operations, ensuring the highest quality of leadership, professionalism and charity. Matthew dedicates himself to making KDT an effective force for good.

**Background:** McCalla is a perennial leader and challenge seeker. After graduating as the salutatorian and president of his high school in Pennsylvania he matriculated at Harvard University. There he graduated with a degree in Comparative Religion with study of Russian and Norwegian language. After Harvard he volunteered for the United States Army, graduated from Officer Candidate School and chose to serve as an Infantry Officer. He deployed once with 10th MTN Division, earned his Ranger Tab, and served as a platoon leader before leaving the service. Matt is an active member of his Church, a perennial student, and avid lifter and martial artist with an interest in language and foreign affairs. He lives to lead in the field and excels at the task.

**Matthew McCalla prioritizes:**
- Relentless precision
- Operational excellence
- "Victory."
