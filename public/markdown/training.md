# KDT Training

Knight Division Tactical offers professional training programs built on Tier 1 operator standards. Courses are open to qualified civilians, military personnel, law enforcement, and corporate security professionals.

## Course Catalog

### Small Arms
Pistol, rifle, and advanced marksmanship training. Courses from fundamentals through advanced tactical marksmanship.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/training/small-arms)

### Tactical Medicine
Tactical Combat Casualty Care (TCCC) and field medical training. Designed for operators, law enforcement, and medical professionals operating in austere environments.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/training/tactical-medicine)

### Executive Protection
Principal protection and close protection courses. Covers threat assessment, protective advances, motorcade operations, and emergency response.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/training/executive-protection)

### Crisis Response
High-risk crisis management, emergency evacuation, and non-permissive environment operations.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/training/crisis-response)

## Who KDT Trains

- Active military personnel seeking advanced skills
- Law enforcement officers
- Corporate security professionals
- Executive protection specialists
- Qualified civilians seeking elite-level training

## Training Philosophy

KDT's training is built on the same standards used internally for Tier 1 operator selection. Classes are small, instructors are Tier 1 operators, and the curriculum is continuously updated based on current operational lessons.
