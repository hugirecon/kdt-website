# KDT Careers

Join the highest echelon of private military operators.

Knight Division Tactical offers career paths across 17 distinct roles. KDT pays industry-leading wages and invests heavily in operator training, technology, and support.

## What KDT Looks For

- **Tier 1 candidates only.** Selection is rigorous.
- **Prior military or equivalent professional experience preferred** for operator roles.
- **Specialist roles open to civilians** with relevant professional qualifications.
- **Integrity, professionalism, and the ability to operate in demanding environments.**

## Operator Roles

- **Knight** — Tier 1 field operator, the core KDT role.
- **Medical Element** — Combat medics and tactical medicine specialists.
- **Communications Element** — Signals, comms, and electronic warfare specialists.
- **Intelligence Unit** — Analysts and all-source intelligence professionals.
- **Pilot** — Rotary and fixed-wing aviation specialists.
- **Drone Operator** — UAV operators and unmanned systems specialists.

## Specialist Roles

- **Account Executive** — Business development and client-facing roles.
- **Propagandist** — Information operations and narrative control.
- **Legal Unit** — In-house counsel, contracts, compliance.
- **Construction & Maintenance** — Facility, vehicle, infrastructure specialists.
- **Manufacturing Unit** — Production and fabrication specialists.
- **Engineering Unit** — Mechanical, electrical, and systems engineers.
- **Logistics** — Supply chain and sustainment specialists.

## Technology Roles

- **AI Developer** — Machine learning engineers for KDT's AI infrastructure.
- **Blockchain Developer** — Blockchain engineers for KDT's records systems.
- **Programmer** — General software engineers.
- **Hacker** — Offensive cyber specialists and red team operators.

## Application Process

KDT runs a structured selection pipeline managed by our Selection Specialist system. The stages include:

1. **Pre-screen** — Baseline qualification check.
2. **Application** — Full application with supporting documentation.
3. **Interview and assessment** — Multiple evaluation stages.
4. **Physical and skill testing** (operator roles).
5. **Background and references.**
6. **Offer and onboarding.**

Decisions are made by KDT's Selection Specialist team. Every candidate gets a structured review.

## Compensation

KDT pays more than any competitor in the private military space. Exact compensation depends on role, experience, and deployment. Industry-leading pay is guaranteed for successful candidates.
