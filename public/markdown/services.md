# KDT Services

Knight Division Tactical offers five core service lines, each staffed by Tier 1 operators and enabled by KDT's proprietary operational infrastructure.

## Service Lines

### 1. Wildlife Mitigation
Anti-poaching operations, conservation security, and wildlife protection. KDT deploys specialized teams to protect endangered species, conservation areas, and wildlife reserves against organized poaching networks.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/services/wildlife-mitigation)

### 2. Direct Action
Offensive operations, target interdiction, and high-risk missions. KDT's direct action teams execute complex operations in permissive and non-permissive environments.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/services/direct-action)

### 3. Counter-Trafficking
Human trafficking disruption, narcotics interdiction, and cross-border operations. KDT partners with law enforcement, NGOs, and governments to dismantle trafficking networks.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/services/counter-trafficking)

### 4. Reconnaissance
Intelligence gathering, surveillance, target development, and area assessment. KDT deploys reconnaissance elements to provide clients with actionable intelligence before they commit resources.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/services/reconnaissance)

### 5. Maritime Operations
Vessel protection, anti-piracy, offshore security, and port operations. KDT maritime teams operate in high-risk waters worldwide.

[Learn more](https://knightdivisiontactical.com/services/maritime-operations)
