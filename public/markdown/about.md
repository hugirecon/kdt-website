## Our Story

Knight Division Tactical was founded to solve a problem that the private military industry had grown comfortable ignoring: inconsistent standards, outdated operational methods, and a willingness to cut corners on training and technology.

KDT was built differently. Co-founded by Michael Schulz (CEO) and Matthew McCalla (COO), the company was architected from day one to operate as a tech-enabled, AI-native private military company. Every system — from candidate selection to deployment logistics — runs on proprietary infrastructure that gives KDT operators an edge no competitor can match.

Today, KDT fields 400+ agents globally and operates across five core service lines, from wildlife conservation security to maritime anti-piracy. Headquartered in Sheridan, Wyoming, KDT is a Tier 1 operator in every sense of the word.

## Our Mission

To deliver the highest echelon of private military services, enabled by one-of-one technology. KDT exists to protect what matters — principals, assets, wildlife, critical infrastructure, and human life — with unmatched professionalism and precision.
