# Knight Division Tactical

**The highest echelon of private military services, enabled by one-of-one technology.**

Knight Division Tactical (KDT) is a Tier 1 private military company delivering unmatched private security, direct action, executive protection, intelligence, and defense technology services.

## Who We Are

KDT was founded by Michael Schulz (CEO) and Matthew McCalla (COO) to build a next-generation private military company — one where every operator, every deployment, and every decision is enabled by proprietary AI and blockchain infrastructure. Headquartered in Sheridan, Wyoming, KDT operates globally.

## What We Do

KDT provides five core service lines:

1. **Wildlife Mitigation** — Anti-poaching, conservation security, wildlife protection operations.
2. **Direct Action** — Offensive operations, target interdiction, high-risk missions.
3. **Counter-Trafficking** — Human trafficking disruption, narcotics interdiction.
4. **Reconnaissance** — Intelligence gathering, surveillance, area assessment.
5. **Maritime Operations** — Vessel protection, anti-piracy, port security.

## What Sets KDT Apart

- **Tier 1 operators only.** KDT's selection pipeline is one of the most rigorous in the private sector.
- **AI-native operations.** Every element of selection, deployment, intelligence, and logistics is powered by KDT's proprietary AI infrastructure.
- **Technology-enabled.** KDT integrates blockchain-based records, AI analysts, and custom operational software across the organization.
- **Industry-leading pay.** KDT pays its operators more than any competitor in the space.
- **400+ agents.** KDT has grown rapidly into a global force.
- **24/7 response-ready.** KDT maintains always-on response capability.
